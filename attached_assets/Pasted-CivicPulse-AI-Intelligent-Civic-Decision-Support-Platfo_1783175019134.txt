CivicPulse AI – Intelligent Civic Decision Support Platform
Objective

Build a production-quality, enterprise-grade, AI-powered web application named CivicPulse AI.

This is NOT a basic complaint portal. It is an AI Decision Intelligence Platform that helps municipalities analyze citizen complaints, prioritize them based on impact, detect duplicate incidents, recommend departments, optimize resources, and provide actionable governance insights.

The application must look like a premium SaaS product suitable for presentation at a Google Hackathon.
